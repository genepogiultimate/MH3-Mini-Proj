**Weapons**

Sword and Shield

The most versatile weapon. Depending on the player hunting style you can play aggressive or safely

Dual Blades

What it lacks in defence makes up for its mobility. Short and quick attacks works great with elemental properties.

Long Sword

Just like a Japanese katana its fluid and swift strikes can build-up devastating comboes with spirit gauge. Take caution as striking also makes you vulnerable.

Great Sword

Explosive raw damage in exchange of its mobility. Timing is precious and can work in your advantage

Hammer

Knock out monsters and gain momentum. Keep pounding that head for "It's Hammer Time". Convinient for breaking monster HARD parts like horns, and armors/scales.

Hunting Horn

Beat em' up with music, literally. The music it emmits give buffs to those who hear it. Some buffs are stackable and goes double time!

Lance

Thrust thrust baby! Behind your trusted shield is where you thrust your lance! Lunge or poke enemies or simply charge your way through.

Gunlance

This short range cannon isn't something funny. It is like a shotgun without the pellet at the tip of the lance. Its terrifying.

Switch Axe

Given from its name it is a weapon default by axe that can transform to sword mode with additional properties depending on its "phial".

Light Bowgun

Running aroung with a bowgun are specialized with rapid fire. single ammo = multiple shots. Cost-efficient right?

Heavy Bowgun

BRING OUT THE BIG GUNS!This one is pretty heavy and can be mounted over crouch when you remove the limiter having a larger clip size.

Bow

A primitive type of weapon that uses coatings boost your shots or add some effects to each shot. Each bow is equipped with 1 type of arc shot (wide,focus,blast) dependent on the weapon crafted.

**ARMOR**


There are 2 types of armor in mh.

"Blademaster"

As it suggests it can only equipped by weilding melee type of weapons. It has a higher raw defense compared to a "gunner". And MOST "blademaster" armor are active with skills related to blademaster weapons.

"Gunner"

*Can only be equipped when using either Bow, Light Bowgun or Heavy Bowgun* As this type of armor may be lacking in raw defense compensates in elemental defense. You will probably notice this as you level your rank through-out the game.
