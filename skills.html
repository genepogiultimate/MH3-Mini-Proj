Skills

Skills are part of your arsenal through-out the game. Skills can empower the weapon you wield,
or simply make healing faster.And those skills have a certain threshold or conditions for it
to activate. Beware as you can also activate negative skills when reaching a negative
threshold( - ). There are a lot of skills that can help you in your quests and there are 3 ways you can possess them:

  • Armor
  • Canteen/Felyne Food buff
  • Talisman
  • Jewel

1st: Most armor parts have embedded skill attributes and the number of skill attributes and skill points vary. Armor have these slots that you can insert jewels in them. There might be some that have the skill attack+2 and health+2. As there is a variety of armors craftable its gonna be a mix and match for every hunter out there.

2nd:As you make your way through the ranks. The chef might make quest's to gather some ingredients to be available when making dishes for you. Skills obtained this way is active for 1 quest. And depending on the ingredients and freshness of it will be the base for the skill you can aquire.

3rd:Talisman will be unlocked after a certain quest. Talisman is like an armor without defense stats, but it does have slots for jewel and an embedded skill to it(random). Talisman can be mined at higher ranks or can be a part of your reward. Every talisman have random skill attributes(max of 2) with random skill points and random jewel slots(max 3).

4th:Jewel can be a part of your quest reward or crafted. Jewels are inserted to jewel slots (weapon , armor , talisman). the main use of these jewel are to add skill points to activate skills or empowered activated skills. Or they can be simply used to negate an active negative skill.
